Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join KESNNUR?

KESNNUR welcomes all nursing students, novice nurses (within 3 years of graduation), and professional nurses who support our mission of empowering future nursing leaders in Kenya.

How much does membership cost?

We offer flexible membership plans: Student (Ksh 100/month), Novice Nurse (Ksh 300/month), and Professional Nurse (Ksh 2,000/year). Annual payments receive a 20% discount.

When will I receive my membership card?

Digital membership cards are available immediately after registration. Physical cards are processed within 2-3 weeks and shipped to your registered address.

Can I upgrade my membership plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade your membership plan at any time. The new rate will be prorated based on your remaining membership period.
