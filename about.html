Who We Are

Kenya Students and Novice Nurses (KESNNUR) is a premier chapter of the National Nurses Association of Kenya (NNAK). We serve as a dynamic platform that brings together all nursing college students, university nursing students, and newly qualified nurses up to five years post-qualification.

Our organization is committed to bridging the gap between nursing education and professional practice. We create opportunities for young nurses to develop essential skills, gain practical experience, and build professional networks that last throughout their careers.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for nursing students and novice nurses in Kenya, empowering them to become competent, compassionate, and innovative healthcare leaders who transform patient care and advance the nursing profession.

Building the future of nursing, one student at a time

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive mentorship, continuous education, and robust networking opportunities that enhance the professional development, well-being, and career progression of nursing students and novice nurses across Kenya.

Empowering nurses for excellence in healthcare delivery

Our Core Values

Guiding principles that define our approach to nursing education and professional development

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming Kenya's leading nursing student organization

Foundation (2022)

KESNNUR was established as a chapter of NNAK with a vision to support nursing students across Kenya.

Initial Growth (2023)

Expanded to 15 regions, launched mentorship programs, and conducted first national conference.

Partnerships (2024)

Established partnerships with leading healthcare institutions and launched digital learning platform.

Current Impact (2025)

Serving 1,500+ members across 24 regions with comprehensive nursing education and support programs.
