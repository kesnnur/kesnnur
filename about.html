About KESNNUR

Who We Are

Kenya Students and Novice Nurses (KESNNUR) is a chapter of the National Nurses Association of Kenya (NNAK). This is a platform which brings together all nursing college and university nursing students as well as newly qualified nurses up to five years post-qualification.

Our mission is to promote professional growth, continuous education, and networking among young nurses in Kenya across different professional interests.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for nursing students and novice nurses in Kenya, empowering them to become competent, compassionate, and innovative healthcare leaders.

Our Mission

To provide mentorship, education, and networking opportunities that enhance the professional development and well-being of nursing students and novice nurses in Kenya.

Our Core Programs

Professional Growth

Mentorship connecting students with experienced nurses

Continuous Education

Workshops, seminars and latest research updates

Mental Health Support

Resources for wellbeing and resilience
