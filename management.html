Governance Structure

Understanding how KESNNUR is organized to effectively serve nursing students and novice nurses across Kenya.

National Level

The National Governing Council sets overall strategy and policies for KESNNUR operations nationwide.

Regional Level

Regional committees implement national policies and coordinate activities within their geographical areas.

Institutional Level

Campus representatives in nursing schools ensure grassroots implementation and member engagement.

Election Process

All leadership positions are democratically elected by KESNNUR members during the Annual General Meeting.
