Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting KESNNUR

How quickly will I get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our main office number.

Can I visit the KESNNUR office?

Yes, our office at Nurses Complex KNH is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We recommend scheduling an appointment in advance.

Who should I contact for membership issues?

For membership applications, renewals, or issues, please email membership@kesnnur.org or contact the Secretary General.

Do you provide emergency support?

For emergencies related to student welfare, please call our emergency hotline at +254 720 000 000, available 24/7.
